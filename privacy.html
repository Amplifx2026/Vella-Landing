AMPLIFX Advertising Agency ("we," "us," "our") operates Vella 2.0, an AI-powered marketing management platform. 5. OAuth Token Security

When you connect your social media accounts, we store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens securely in our encrypted database. These tokens allow Vella 2.0 to act on your behalf (post content, read analytics, manage campaigns). You can revoke access at any time by disconnecting your accounts in Settings, which immediately deletes the stored tokens from our system.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database encryption at rest via Supabase
  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt
  • JWT-based authentication with token expiration
  • API keys stored as environment variables, never exposed to clients
  • OAuth tokens stored encrypted in our database

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion or subscription cancellation, we will delete your personal data and OAuth tokens within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics and service improvement purposes.

8. Cookies

Vella 2.0 uses essential cookies and local storage for authentication (JWT tokens) and session management. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies. No data is shared with advertising networks.
